About Our Team

We are a team of data scientists passionate about leveraging deep learning techniques for medical advancements. Our project, developed as part of the Infyma Hackathon, focuses on the early detection of retinopathy using advanced machine learning algorithms. By combining cutting-edge research with practical implementation, we aim to enhance diagnostic accuracy and support medical professionals in making faster, more reliable decisions.

Our Mission

Our goal is to develop an intelligent system capable of identifying retinopathy at an early stage, helping reduce preventable vision loss. By utilizing deep learning models trained on high-quality medical imaging data, we strive to create a tool that assists ophthalmologists in detecting retinal abnormalities with precision and efficiency.

Our Team

Our team consists of dedicated data scientists with a strong foundation in machine learning, deep learning, and data analysis. We work collaboratively to preprocess data, train models, and fine-tune performance to achieve optimal results.

About Us

We are Data Science students at COMSATS with expertise in machine learning and deep learning. Our academic background, combined with hands-on experience in real-world projects, has equipped us with the ability to solve complex problems in the medical domain. We are driven by a shared commitment to innovation, research, and impactful solutions.

Our retinopathy detection project is designed to bridge the gap between AI and healthcare, making diagnosis more accessible and efficient.
